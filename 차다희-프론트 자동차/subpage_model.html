Bentley Flying Spur Hybrid: The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Efficiency

The Bentley Flying Spur Hybrid is a remarkable combination of refined luxury and cutting-edge hybrid technology. Designed for those who desire both top-tier performance and environmental consciousness, the Flying Spur Hybrid offers an uncompromising driving experience while reducing its carbon footprint.


Performance and Efficiency

At the heart of the Flying Spur Hybrid is a 2.9-liter V6 petrol engine paired with an advanced electric motor, together delivering a total output of 536 horsepower and 750 Nm of torque. This hybrid system allows for smooth, powerful acceleration, taking the car from 0 to 60 mph in just 4.1 seconds, providing an exhilarating driving experience that Bentley is renowned for.


The electric motor allows for emissions-free driving over short distances, making it perfect for city driving. In fully electric mode, the Flying Spur Hybrid can travel up to 25 miles (around 40 kilometers) on a single charge, ideal for short commutes or urban travel. Combined with the petrol engine, it offers a total driving range of over 400 miles (about 644 kilometers), ensuring convenience for long-distance travel.


Advanced Hybrid Technology

The Flying Spur Hybrid seamlessly transitions between electric and petrol power, optimizing efficiency without compromising performance. Drivers can choose between three driving modes: EV Drive, Hybrid Mode, and Hold Mode.


EV Drive focuses solely on the electric motor for zero-emission journeys.
Hybrid Mode intelligently combines the electric motor and the petrol engine, selecting the most efficient power source based on driving conditions.
Hold Mode conserves the battery for later use, ideal when you need electric power for city driving after a highway journey.


Luxury Meets Sustainability

As with all Bentley models, the interior of the Flying Spur Hybrid is crafted with the finest materials, featuring exquisite leather, sustainable wood veneers, and advanced technology for the ultimate in comfort and convenience. Despite its hybrid powertrain, the Flying Spur retains the elegance, luxury, and craftsmanship that define the Bentley brand.

Environmentally Conscious Luxury

The Flying Spur Hybrid demonstrates Bentley’s commitment to sustainability as part of its Beyond100 strategy, where the brand aims to become carbon neutral by 2030. The hybrid technology reduces CO2 emissions while delivering Bentley’s signature driving performance.
